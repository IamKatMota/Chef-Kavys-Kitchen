Meet Chef Kavy

Cooking discovered me, igniting a passion that has driven my journey ever since. In 2014, I launched my catering business in Los Angeles, and each year since, I’ve had the privilege to watch it grow, allowing me to work for—and alongside—incredible people.

After relocating to New Orleans, I had the unique honor of cooking for several New Orleans Saints players over the course of four years. This experience deepened my appreciation for Creole cuisine and broadened my culinary techniques, further refining my craft.

My mission is to create a welcoming, family-friendly dining experience, a place where patrons come together over soulful meals that feel like home. Built through the trust of our valued guests and the power of word-of-mouth, we are deeply grateful for our customers’ support.

We embrace feedback as a guiding force, consistently refining our offerings to ensure our guests leave with lasting smiles. Thank you for being an essential part of this flavorful journey.
